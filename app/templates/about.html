Sizing Guide

Tops use standard sizing: S (36-38 chest), M (38-40), L (40-42), XL (42-44). Bottoms use waist sizing 28-36. If between sizes, choose one size up for a relaxed fit.

Returns

Returns are accepted within 30 days for unworn items with original tags. Contact support@looph.co with your order ID to begin. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after inspection.

FAQ

Shipping: Metro areas: 2-4 days, provincial: 4-7 days. Payments: COD and GCash are supported. Order changes: Allowed within 1 hour after placing order.
